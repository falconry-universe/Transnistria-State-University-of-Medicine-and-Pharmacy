About

Founded in 1995, State University of Medicine and Pharmacy "Igor Smirnov" has a tradition of academic excellence.

Mission: To provide top-tier medical theory education via the internet.

Vision: A world-class online institution.

Why "Igor Smirnov"? Named in honor of Igor Smirnov, a significant figure from Transnistria, to inspire leadership and a commitment to public service.

Course Overview

Medicine

  • Curriculum: Anatomy, Physiology, etc.
  • Duration: 6 years
  • Fees: $10,000 per annum

Pharmacy

  • Curriculum: Pharmacology, Medicinal Chemistry, etc.
  • Duration: 5 years
  • Fees: $9,000 per annum

Student Life

  • Facilities: Labs, libraries, sports
  • Clubs: Medical Journal Club, Pharmacy Society
  • City Life: Rich culture, vibrant nightlife

Admission Requirements

Medicine

  • High school diploma, Biology and Chemistry
  • Entrance exam
  • Working internet connection

Pharmacy

  • High school diploma, Math and Chemistry
  • Interview process
  • Working internet connection
